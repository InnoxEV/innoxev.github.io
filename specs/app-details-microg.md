# MicroG App Details Page Spec

## Goal
Provide a clear, trustworthy, and practical guide for using MicroG to enable Google services and make apps like Google Maps work correctly.

---

## Page Path
/en/apps/microg.html

---

## Page Structure

### 1. Header

- App Name: MicroG
- Tag: Utility
- Short Description:
  "Enable Google services without Google Play Services"

---

### 2. Problem Section

Title: "The Problem"

Content:
Google apps (like Google Maps) do not work properly without Google Play Services.

Many users using modified systems or APK setups face issues like:
- Apps crashing
- Location not working
- Login failures

---

### 3. Solution Section

Title: "The Solution"

Content:
MicroG replaces Google Play Services and allows apps to function normally.

It enables:
- Location services
- Google account support
- App compatibility

---

### 4. Key Features

- Lightweight alternative to Google Play Services
- Supports Google login
- Enables location services
- Works with apps like Google Maps

---

### 5. How It Works (Important Section)

Explain clearly:

MicroG acts as a replacement layer for Google Play Services.

Apps think Google services are installed, allowing them to function normally.

2. Open MicroG settings

### 8. Common Issues

- MicroG not working → Check permissions and settings
- Location not working → Enable location services
- Google Maps not opening → Reinstall or clear app data
