MicroG

Enable Google services without Google Play Services

The Problem

Google apps (like Google Maps) do not work properly without Google Play Services.

Many users using modified systems or APK setups face issues like:

  • Apps crashing
  • Location not working
  • Login failures

The Solution

MicroG replaces Google Play Services and allows apps to function normally.

It enables:

  • Location services
  • Google account support
  • App compatibility

Key Features

  • Lightweight alternative to Google Play Services
  • Supports Google login
  • Enables location services
  • Works with apps like Google Maps

How It Works

MicroG acts as a replacement layer for Google Play Services.

Apps think Google services are installed, allowing them to function normally.

Setup Guide

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Install MicroG APK
  2. Open MicroG settings
  3. Enable required permissions
  4. Configure Google services
  5. Install Google Maps
  6. Test navigation and location
