Google Maps

Navigation and location services with MicroG support

The Problem

Google Maps may not work properly without Google Play Services.

Users may face:

  • App not opening
  • Location not working
  • Navigation errors

The Solution

Using MicroG allows Google Maps to function normally without Google Play Services.

Key Features

  • Turn-by-turn navigation
  • Real-time location tracking
  • Search for places
  • Route planning

How It Works

MicroG replaces Google Play Services, allowing Google Maps to function normally.
